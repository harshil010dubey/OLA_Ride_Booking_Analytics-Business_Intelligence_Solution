# OLA_Ride_Booking_Analytics-Business_Intelligence_Solution
Analyzed 103K+ ride-booking records using SQL, Excel, and Power BI to uncover insights on revenue, cancellations, customer behavior, and driver performance. Built interactive dashboards with 20+ KPIs, identified operational bottlenecks, and generated data-driven recommendations to improve efficiency, customer satisfaction, and revenue growth.

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end data analytics solution built to analyze OLA ride-booking operations and generate actionable business insights. The project leverages **Excel, SQL, and Power BI** to transform raw ride-booking data into meaningful visualizations and business intelligence reports.

The analysis focuses on customer behavior, ride completion rates, cancellations, revenue performance, payment preferences, and driver performance to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

OLA operates in a highly competitive ride-hailing market where operational efficiency and customer satisfaction directly impact revenue.

Key business questions addressed:

* What is the ride completion rate?
* What factors contribute to ride cancellations?
* Which vehicle categories generate the highest revenue?
* What are customer payment preferences?
* How satisfied are customers and drivers?
* How can operational performance be improved?

## 💡 Key Insights

* Generated **₹35.08 Million** in revenue from successful rides.
* Ride completion rate stands at **62.09%**.
* Driver-related issues contributed significantly to booking failures.
* Prime Sedan emerged as the highest revenue-generating vehicle category.
* UPI and Cash were the most preferred payment methods.
* Customer satisfaction remained consistently high with an average rating of **4.0/5**.
